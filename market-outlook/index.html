Weekly Performance Summary

The first week of January 2025 showed mixed signals across global markets. US equities started strong with the S&P 500 gaining 1.2%, while European markets faced headwinds from ongoing economic concerns.

Key Economic Data

  • US Non-Farm Payrolls: +185,000 (above expectations)
  • Unemployment Rate: 3.7% (stable)
  • Consumer Confidence Index: 108.5 (increase from 107.2)
  • Manufacturing PMI: 48.9 (slight contraction)

Market Sectors Performance

Technology stocks led gains with NASDAQ up 1.8%, while energy sectors declined 2.1% due to falling oil prices. Financial services showed modest gains of 0.8%.

Looking Ahead

Next week's focus will be on inflation data and Federal Reserve minutes. Market participants will be watching for any signals regarding interest rate policy.
